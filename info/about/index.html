Hello! My name is Igor. I'm studying A Course in Miracles. And if you are reading these lines most likely you are aware what a challenge it is to learn this book. After two years of my fruitless attempts to understand the Course I met Gary Renard's book The Disappearance of the Universe which allowed me to expand my mind wide enough to the point where I could grasp ACIM's message.

Now I write my own short notes which I wish I had when I was new to ACIM. I'm focusing mostly on the very first section of the Text Principles of Miracles because I remember how impossible it was for me personally to understand it. I had been using a website http://hypothes.is for writing my notes right on ACIM's web pages, but as the number of notes increased I decided to build this little website to have all of my notes in one place. It will be my pleasure If you will find them interesting. Also I couldn't find a source of random quotes from the book to read at the end of the acim study groups' sessions, so called ACIM Fortune Cookies. As a result of all this, on this website you can read my notes about the Course as well as get in touch with other ACIM students by joining online study groups and taste some fortune cookies, enjoy ;)

ACIMnotes' Facebook page will let you know about the updates. Please use the Send Message button there to contact me.
